Welcome to Baba Amreshwar Dham

Introduction

Baba Amareshwar Temple, a sacred abode of Lord Shiva is located in Angrabari Village near Khunti District of the State of Jharkhand, which is a tribal dominated area. This temple is the second largest and important abode of Lord Shiva in Jharkhand after Deoghar. The Shivlingam worshipped in the temple is Swayambhu- appeared on its own. Amereshwar Dham is situated at a distance of 45 kilometers from Ranchi the capital of Jharkhand.
